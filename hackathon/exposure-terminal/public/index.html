# Producer Laws · States Annual fee exposure ↓ Max penalty / day Nearest deadline Exposure
How to read this. “Annual fee exposure” is a producer’s portfolio-wide fee range across every enacted law it’s exposed to — grounded in published fee schedules where the grounded tag shows, benchmark estimates otherwise. “Max penalty / day” is the largest single statutory civil penalty in that portfolio — a ceiling if in continuous default, not added into the fee number. These are exposure ceilings, not booked losses — the way a short thesis is framed, not an audited figure.
