How the read works. Every number comes straight from the public GET /insights/champions feed — sponsorship counts, enacted totals, per-legislator instrument & material specialties. The advocacy bucket and influence score are this tool's own layer, computed in a ~150-line zero-dependency proxy: influence = enacted×6 + primary×1 + cosponsor×0.4 (landing law counts for far more than filing it). CLOSER = 2+ enacted · WORKHORSE = 6+ bills, <2 landed · RISER = emerging. Click track record to pull a legislator's bill-by-bill receipts from /insights/champions/{id}/bills. Not vibes — grounded.
